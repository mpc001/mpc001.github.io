About Me

I'm a third-year Ph.D. student at Intelligent Behaviour Understanding Group (IBUG) under the supervision of Prof. Maja Pantic and Dr. Stavros Petridis. My Ph.D. research topic is end-to-end audiovisual speech recognition.

More about me: Google Scholar, Github and Instagram.
